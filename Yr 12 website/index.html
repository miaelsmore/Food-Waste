What Is Love Food Hate Waste?

    Love Food Hate Waste is a healthy food website that includes alternate recipes that not only tastes amazing but it also makes you feel amazing! There are recipes that you can follow and images that you can relate to. The information page give you tips and helpful information to help you to not only make healthy and alternate meals/snacks, it gives you ways to maintain eating healthy in your everyday lives. If you view the interview page you can get an insight into the one and only famous cheif Jaime Oliver. You can learn about how Jaime Oliver's career started and his background. There are two main kinds of wasted food: food loss and food waste. Food loss is the bigger category, and incorporates any edible food that goes uneaten at any stage.
